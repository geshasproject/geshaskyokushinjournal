The Gesha’s Kyokushin Journal

is an online newspaper for the development of Masutatsu Oyama’s Way, The Kyokushinkai Karate, through an independent personal approach as well as independent from any organisation, Federation and other standpoints. Its purpose is not piling up followers, rather encouraging for constructive, critical thinking, by sharing science, sports science, self-defense, martial arts, karate and Kyokushin Karate related studies, articles and other materials.

"Reading good books implants good ideas in the mind, develops good aspirations, and leads to the cultivation of good friends."

-Masutatsu Oyama-
